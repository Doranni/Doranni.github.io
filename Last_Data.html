Last Data

You can see the progress with the game on GitHub.

Space mini game. Development is still in progress.

When you have your last days before the death of your space system, what will you choose? What last data about humanity will you decide to save?

Already implemented:

You can fire or avoid asteroids. Colliding with them causes damage. With firing and flying you spend laser charge and fuel. Flying faster helps to spend less fuel.
Space objects, available for interaction, are outlined with different colors, based on their distance to the ship.
